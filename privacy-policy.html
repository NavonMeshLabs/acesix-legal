Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AceSix ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the AceSix mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including subjects accessed, quiz results, and session duration.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status and transaction history processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store your payment card details.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymised data to help us understand app performance and improve the user experience.

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details such as your name or email address to use AceSix.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the AceSix app and its features
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Track your learning progress within the app
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs
  • Understand how users interact with the app to improve content
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

AceSix uses the following third-party services which may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Payment processing for subscriptions.
  • Expo / React Native: App development framework that may collect crash and performance data.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain usage and analytics data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

AceSix is an educational app designed for students aged 14 and above (GCSE level). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (UK: ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Subscriptions

AceSix offers auto-renewable subscriptions (Monthly and Annual). Subscriptions are managed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your device's subscription settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
